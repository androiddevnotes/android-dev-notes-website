# Schedule Local Push Notification that works even when app is in background with AlarmManager, BroadcastReceiver and NotificationManager

## Overview

The app demos a specific functionality of how to schedule local push notifications that work even when the app is in the background. The app uses the AlarmManager, BroadcastReceiver and NotificationManager to schedule and display the notifications.

## Tech Stack

- AlarmManager
- BroadcastReceiver
- NotificationManager
- Calendar
- ViewBinding
